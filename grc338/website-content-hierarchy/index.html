Welcome to Frog's Cafe!

Frog's Cafe is a wonderful combination of scratch made pastries, dark roasted coffee, and tasty breakfast items served with a smile in the heart of San Luis Obispo. Our beautiful space is perfect for studying, socializing, relaxing, and creating memories. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we have limited outdoor seating and shortened hours, however we are still doing takeout and phone orders. We thank you for coming by and making us your coffee and breakfast destination in the beautiful city of SLO.
