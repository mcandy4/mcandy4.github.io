Introduction

Sage Boutique

A sustainable, ethical, and women-owned clothing company that uses eco-friendly fabrics like organic cotton, linen, and various recycled materials, while also prioritizing safe and fair treatment to their workers.

Target audience

The main target audience will consist mostly of women who care about reducing their carbon footprint and supporting sustainable businesses, but still want to wear comfortable, affordable, and stylish clothes. Audience could be a wide variety of people, as we have clothing for younger generations, older generations, plus size, and kids clothing.

Their primary goals when shopping at Sage Boutique are to have a unique and inclusive online experience, and have security knowing that they are shopping ethically and sustainably. Their priorities lie in supporting local, sustainable businesses that care about the environment and supporting all types of women.
